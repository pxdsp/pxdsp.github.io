Emma

Meet Emma – my dear friend and hands-down my favorite model to work with. We’ve known each other for five years, and in that time, we’ve collaborated on countless shoots. What makes our partnership so special is that Emma is not just a model; she’s also a talented photographer. This unique dynamic allows us to bounce creative ideas off each other, bringing fresh energy and perspectives to every session. Over the years, we've explored a variety of locations, each one adding its own flavor to our work. From the Ostend beach, to Emma’s charming Art Nouveau apartment and my studio, where we experiment with different lighting and setups, we’ve found endless inspiration in our surroundings. For our shoots, I typically use the Canon 6D Mark II, paired with a 2.8f zoom lens, ensuring every shot is crisp, detailed, and full of depth.
